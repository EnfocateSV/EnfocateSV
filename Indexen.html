About Us

About Us

We are a non-profit organization, made up of 14 young agents of change, who seek to promote the personal and academic development of young people, and then guide them to entrepreneurship, based on the Sustainable Development Goals.

Mission

To be an organization that projects commitment and leadership, by contributing to the training of leaders, agents of change and volunteers, who, based on the Sustainable Development Goals, seek to generate opportunities for personal, academic and entrepreneurial training, making a positive impact in the life of youth.

Vision

Strengthen personal and academic growth in young people to a desire to overcome themselves. Forming soft skills and positive values, thus creating leaders, agents of change and volunteers in their communities, to later guide them to the world of work, through entrepreneurship, with a focus on Sustainable developement goals.

Services

What we offer


At Enfócate SV we provide free training opportunities in the areas of personal, academic, work and mathematics development for young Salvadorans in Latin America and the Caribbean, through different webinars, workshops, forums, diploma courses and others in virtual modality, as well as blended learning. We also offer the opportunity to volunteer with us, to continue strengthening agents of change, in order to generate a positive impact.

In addition, we offer scholarships to young Salvadorans to take our "Entrepreneurial Leaders" diploma course.
